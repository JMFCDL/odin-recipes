Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

The classic chocolate chip cookie. This cookie is known to be chewy and with every bite, the flavor is rich and buttery. The chocolate chips also give a welcomed sweet surprise.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking Soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture
  3. Stir in the flour and chocolate chips
  4. Put the dough onto a prepared baking sheet
  5. In an oven preheated to 350 degree F, 10 minutes is all it takes, or until the edges are golden brown
